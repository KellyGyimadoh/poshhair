About Us

Posh Hair Dock is your one-stop shop for the highest quality hair extensions and wigs. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best hair products, unparalleled customer service, and a shopping experience that is both enjoyable and easy.

Our team of experts is dedicated to helping you find the perfect hair solutions that fit your style and preferences. We offer a wide range of products including human hair wigs, synthetic wigs, lace front wigs, hair bundles, and more.
